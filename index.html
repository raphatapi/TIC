About True Impact Consulting

True Impact was founded in 2015 and started by helping a single customer with a single retail location improve their operations, open a second location and launch a franchise division. Today this concept has 6 locations and over 10 franchises sold and is growing rapidly. Over the years we have helped business leaders make acquisitions, launch new offerings, define process and procedure for business development and even exit a money losing investment at minimal cost. Additionally, True Impact has a wide network of expert solution partners that we bring to help meet your needs.

Key Services:

  • Management Consulting – Solutions tailored to overcome the unique operational or team challenges you are facing
  • Business Development – Growing your business through unique, cost effective strategies
  • Marketing Consulting – Helping the entrepreneur define and crystalize their product or service messaging and getting the word out
